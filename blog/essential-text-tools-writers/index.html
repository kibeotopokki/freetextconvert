1. Case Converter: The Swiss Army Knife of Text Formatting

Every writer encounters situations where text is in the wrong case. Maybe you accidentally left CAPS LOCK on, or you need to format a headline properly. A good case converter is indispensable.

Why Writers Need It:

  • Headlines and Titles: Convert to proper Title Case for blogs and articles
  • Social Media: Switch between casual lowercase and attention-grabbing UPPERCASE
  • Technical Writing: Format variable names (camelCase, snake_case)
  • Email Subject Lines: Test different cases for better open rates

Real-World Example:

Input: "the ultimate guide to CONTENT MARKETING in 2025"

Output: "The Ultimate Guide to Content Marketing in 2025"

Pro Tip: Use sentence case for body text and title case for headlines. Studies show that title case headlines get 15% more clicks than sentence case.
