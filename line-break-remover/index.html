About the Line Break Remover Tool

Our free online line break remover helps you clean up text that has unwanted line breaks, often from copying content from PDFs, emails, or other documents. This tool instantly removes or normalizes line breaks while preserving your intended formatting.

Features:

  • Remove all line breaks to create continuous text
  • Remove only single line breaks while preserving paragraphs
  • Replace multiple line breaks with single ones
  • Remove extra spaces and clean up formatting
  • Preserve paragraph structure when needed

Perfect for students, writers, researchers, and anyone who frequently copies text between different applications and needs to fix formatting issues quickly.
